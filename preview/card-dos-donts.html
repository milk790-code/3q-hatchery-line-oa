Standards · Do / Don't

Standards

  • negative space above 50%
  • single soft light source
  • low saturation · high tonal contrast
  • film grain visible
  • gold used as hairline only (under 3%)
  • central axis alignment

Banned

  • bright / champagne / rose gold
  • gradient metallics
  • marble or stock textures
  • emoji · unicode icons
  • HDR · vignette · sharpening
  • stock handshake or particle photos
